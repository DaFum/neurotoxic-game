# Performance Budget Enforcer

## Workflow

1. Identify current performance constraints (bundle size, load time).
2. Propose measurable budgets and checks.
3. Suggest optimizations (code splitting, lazy loading, asset compression).

## Output

- Provide recommended budgets and next steps.
