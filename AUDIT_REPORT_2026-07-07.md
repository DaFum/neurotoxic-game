# Neurotoxic Codebase Audit — Logic & Feature Review (2026-07-07)

**Scope:** read-only audit of `src/` (plus `public/locales/` for i18n), verified against root and nested `AGENTS.md` conventions. Every finding was confirmed by reading the code, not just grep hits. No source files were modified by the audit itself.
**Method:** fresh `symbols.json` (1,743 symbols / 674 files), six parallel verified sweeps (duplicates, orphans, state/payload safety, i18n, style/pattern, dead-code/integration), each Grep-verifying every claim.
**Predecessor:** `AUDIT_REPORT.md` (2026-07-04) — its fix pass landed; this audit found none of its resolved items regressed.
**Overall verdict:** the codebase is unusually clean — zero true orphans, zero stale reducer cases, full EN/DE locale parity, no color/audio-timing/React-19 violations. Actionable findings concentrate in a few sanitizer stragglers, one HUD duplication, and a batch of dead or unwired locale keys.

**Policy for this report:** where a finding could be resolved by either integration or deletion, the recommended action is **INTEGRATE / WIRE-UP**, not DELETE.
